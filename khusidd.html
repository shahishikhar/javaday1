Sajjan Raj Vaidya

          Sajjan Raj Vaidya is a Nepali 
          singer-songwriter, guitarist and producer.
          Sajjan grew up writing and playing music 
          from a young age often playing
          live shows in his hometown, Kathmandu, 
          as a singer-songwriter. Sajjan
          initially received recognition through 
          his works on YouTube and
          SoundCloud. He released his 
          first official song “Aaja Matra” on SoundCloud
          and YouTube in 2014.
